Overview

This is an introductory course in Indian Sign Language. It will cover topics beginning with the alphabet, numbers and basic words and progressing to colors, relations, days of the week, months, and standard signs. It consists of 11 lessons taught by Pragya and will help you get started in understanding Indian Sign Language and communicate with the deaf community.

Syllabus

  • Indian Sign Language - Introduction
    • Overview of the course
    • Introduction to Indian Sign Language
  • Indian Sign Language - History and Importance
    • History of ISL
    • Importance of ISL
  • Indian Sign Language - Video Lessons
    • Alphabet and Common Phrases Video Lessons
